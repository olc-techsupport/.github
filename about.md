## Our Project
Communities across the globe are navigating environmental disruption, including:
- Air and water contamination  
- Declining freshwater availability  
- Habitat loss  
- Climate change  

For Tribal Nations, these challenges are compounded by systemic inequities and limited access to data tools.

At the same time, Tribal communities hold deep knowledge rooted in:
- Balance  
- Reciprocity  
- Long-term stewardship  

## The Integrated Data Cube (IDC)/Cubedynamics Project

The Project is a:
- **Data platform** for environmental analysis  
- **Learning environment** for students and communities  
- **Bridge** between Earth data science and Traditional Knowledges  

It supports communities in gathering, interpreting, and applying environmental data in ways aligned with their governance systems.

## Our Vision
To strengthen Tribal sovereignty and resilience by ensuring that environmental decisions are guided by:
- Tribal leadership  
- Community knowledge  
- Accessible, high-quality data tools
